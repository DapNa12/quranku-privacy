# Privacy Policy for QuranKu Mobile

**Last updated: July 2026**

Welcome to **QuranKu Mobile** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Location Data
Our app requests and uses your device's location data (Fine and Coarse Location) **solely** for the purpose of:
1. Calculating accurate prayer times (Jadwal Sholat) based on your current geographical coordinates.
2. Determining the correct Qibla direction (Arah Kiblat) relative to your position.

**Important Note:** Your location data is processed locally on your device or sent securely to reliable third-party APIs (such as Aladhan API) to fetch calculation data. We **do not** store, track, sell, or share your continuous location history on our own servers.

### B. Device Storage (Local Data)
We use your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) to save your preferences, such as:
- App theme (Light/Dark mode)
- Bookmarks and "Last Read" (Terakhir Dibaca) history
- Dzikir (Tasbih) counter progress
- Tahfidz mode difficulty and progress

This data remains on your device unless you actively choose to sync it to our cloud database.

### C. Cloud Storage & Account Data (Optional)
If you choose to create an account and log in, we collect your email address and securely store your synced bookmarks and reading history in our database (powered by Supabase) so you can access them across multiple devices.

## 2. Third-Party Services
Our application utilizes third-party APIs and services to function properly:
- **Al Quran Cloud API & eQuran.id**: To fetch Quranic texts, translations, and audio (murottal).
- **Aladhan API**: To fetch prayer times.
- **OpenRouter (AI Chat)**: To process queries sent to our Islamic AI Assistant. Only the questions you type into the chat feature are sent to this service.

These services have their own privacy policies governing the data they process.

## 3. Data Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. 

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. 

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
